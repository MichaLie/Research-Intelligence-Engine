## Key Evidence Findings (Round 1)

### Critical Localization Data
- **PIN5**: Endoplasmic reticulum (ER) membrane - CONFIRMED
- **NRAMP6**: Golgi/trans-Golgi network (TGN) - CONFIRMED (Li 2019)
- **Implication**: These proteins are in DIFFERENT subcellular compartments; direct ER-based coordination is unlikely

### Substrate Specificity
- **PIN5**: Auxin (IAA) - transports into ER lumen
- **NRAMP6**: Cadmium (Cd2+), Manganese (Mn2+), possibly Iron (Fe2+)

### Crosstalk Evidence
- Strong evidence for auxin-heavy metal crosstalk at systems level
- No direct PIN5-NRAMP6 interaction documented
- Phloem contains multiple metal-binding/transport proteins (NaKR1, OPT3)

## Key Evidence Findings (Round 2)

### New Critical Findings

1. **DAO requires Fe(II) cofactor** (Hayashi 2021, Jin 2020)
   - DAO is main auxin catabolic enzyme
   - Fe(II) is essential for activity
   - Supports indirect metal-auxin link: if NRAMP6 provides cytosolic Fe, DAO could be affected

2. **NRAMP2 paradigm for TGN NRAMPs** (Alejandro 2017)
   - NRAMP2 is TGN-localized (like NRAMP6)
   - Provides cytosolic Mn pool for SOD and chloroplasts
   - Acts INDIRECTLY on downstream compartments via cytosolic metal pools
   - Model for how NRAMP6 could affect ER/cytosolic processes without direct co-localization

3. **APL is master companion cell TF** (Bonke 2003, Abe 2015)
   - Promotes phloem, represses xylem
   - Regulates BOTH molecules AND their transport machinery (FT + FTIP1)
   - Candidate for H4 (shared transcription factors)

4. **Phloem parenchyma is APL-independent** (Kim 2021)
   - Complicates H4 if PIN5/NRAMP6 are PP-expressed rather than CC-expressed

5. **NRAMP6 regulated by Ca2+, not auxin** (Zhang 2023)
   - CPK21/23 kinases phosphorylate NRAMP6
   - COUNTER-EVIDENCE for direct auxin-NRAMP6 functional link

6. **PIN5 has minor phenotypes** (Chen 2013, Ganguly 2010)
   - Redundancy with PIN8 and PILS family
   - COUNTER-EVIDENCE for strong PIN5 function

| Are promoter motifs characterized? | **NO** | Dof + HD-ZIP paradigm exists (Schneidereit 2008), not applied to PIN5/NRAMP6 |

### Expression Pattern Summary

**PIN5** (AT5G57090):
- Root and hypocotyl vasculature (Mravec 2009)
- Pavement and guard cells of cotyledon (Mravec 2009)
- Involved in vein patterning (Sawchuk & Scarpella 2013)
- Subcellular localization varies by cell type (ER in vascular, PM in epidermis)
- **CC-specific expression: NOT DOCUMENTED**

**NRAMP6** (AT1G15960):
- Higher in shoots than roots (Li 2019)
- Low in main root; strong in lateral root stele (Li 2019)
- Confined to major vascular tissues in leaves (Li 2019)
- Not detected in LRP or immature lateral roots (Li 2019)
- **CC-specific expression: NOT DOCUMENTED**

### Companion Cell Promoter Paradigm

From Schneidereit et al. 2008 (SUC2 study):
- **Dof motifs**: Core sequence 5'-(T/A)AAAG-3' - required for CC expression
- **HD-ZIP motifs**: Co-required with Dof
- Simultaneous deletion of Dof + HD-ZIP abolishes CC-specific expression
- >50% of Dof TFs expressed in vascular system
- **Testable prediction**: PIN5 and NRAMP6 promoters may share Dof/HD-ZIP motifs if H4 is correct

### Critical Gaps Remaining

1. **Co-expression validation** - CANNOT be completed through literature; requires computational query of scRNA-seq databases
2. **APL binding to promoters** - No ChIP-seq data exists; requires bioinformatic promoter analysis
3. **CC-specific expression** - Neither gene has documented CC-specific localization
